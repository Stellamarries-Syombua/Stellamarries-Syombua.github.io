Machine Learning · Healthcare

Patient Risk Prediction System

End-to-end ML pipeline classifying diabetes risk from structured clinical data. Covers the full data science lifecycle; from raw data auditing and EDA through model selection, threshold tuning, and SHAP explainability.

Data Auditing & EDA
Identified zeros, outliers, and a 48.7% missing rate in insulin — critical gap for real-world deployment.
Model Training & Selection
Compared Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting; GBM selected with AUC 0.821.
Explainability & Risk Tiers
SHAP values rank glucose as #1 predictor. Threshold tuned to 0.35 to prioritise clinical recall.

Clinical Data Analysis · Kenya

Breast Cancer Molecular Subtype Distribution

Analysed IHC marker data from 167 breast cancer patients across 3 Kenyan hospital sites (TACA Study) to characterise molecular subtype distribution; finding 19.4% Triple Negative prevalence in a population with limited prior profiling.

Data Preparation & IHC Filtering
Filtered 167 DSQ participants to 144 IHC records across Kenya-KTRH, MERU, and NTRH.
Molecular Subtype Classification
Classified 134 patients into 5 subtypes using ER, PR, and HER2 IHC surrogate markers.
Cross-Site Analysis & Completeness Audit
Stratified subtype distributions by site and audited IHC data completeness (88–95%) per hospital.
